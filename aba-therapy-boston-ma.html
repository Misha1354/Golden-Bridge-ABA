FAQ

ABA Therapy in Boston — Common Questions

What Boston neighborhoods do you serve for in-home ABA therapy?

We provide in-home ABA therapy throughout Boston, including Back Bay, South End, Dorchester, Roxbury, Jamaica Plain, Allston, Brighton, Charlestown, East Boston, West Roxbury, Hyde Park, Mattapan, and the Fenway.

Do you work with Boston Public Schools (BPS)?

Yes. Our BCBAs and RBTs collaborate with Boston Public Schools and private schools across the city to support students with autism in the classroom, with goals aligned to each student's IEP.

Is ABA therapy in Boston covered by MassHealth?

Yes. MassHealth covers ABA therapy for children with autism throughout Boston. We're also in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield of MA, Tufts Health Plan, Harvard Pilgrim, Aetna, and Cigna. We verify your benefits at no cost.

How quickly can my child start ABA therapy in Boston?

After a free consultation, intake and insurance verification typically take 1–3 weeks. Once approved, we match your child with a Boston-area therapist and begin sessions on your schedule.
