ABA Therapy in Framingham — Common Questions

What MetroWest towns near Framingham do you serve?

We provide in-home and in-school ABA therapy in Framingham and across MetroWest, including Natick, Wayland, Sudbury, Marlborough, Ashland, Hopkinton, Holliston, Sherborn, and Southborough.

Do you work with Framingham Public Schools?

Yes. Our BCBAs and RBTs collaborate with Framingham Public Schools and private schools across MetroWest to support students with autism, with goals aligned to each student's IEP.

Is ABA therapy in Framingham covered by MassHealth?

Yes. MassHealth covers ABA therapy for children with autism throughout Framingham and MetroWest. We're also in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield of MA, Tufts Health Plan, Harvard Pilgrim, Aetna, and Cigna. We verify your benefits at no cost.

How quickly can my child start ABA therapy in Framingham?

After a free consultation, intake and insurance verification typically take 1–3 weeks. Once approved, we match your child with a MetroWest-area therapist and begin sessions on your schedule.
