Who We Are

Connecting Families to the Care They Deserve

At Golden Bridge ABA, we believe every child has the potential to grow, communicate, and connect. Our team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) design individualized programs grounded in the science of Applied Behavior Analysis.

We meet children where they are — at home and at school — partnering closely with families and educators to build meaningful, lasting skills.

  • ✓ Individualized treatment plans
  • ✓ BCBA-supervised programs
  • ✓ Family training & collaboration
  • ✓ Regular progress monitoring
What We Offer

Our Services

We provide ABA therapy in the settings that matter most for your child's development.

🏠

In-Home ABA Therapy

Therapy delivered in the comfort of your home, where your child feels most at ease. Our therapists work within your family's natural routines to build skills that generalize across everyday settings.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Parent training included
  • Naturalistic teaching strategies
  • Sibling & family involvement
🏫

In-School ABA Therapy

Our therapists collaborate directly with teachers and school staff to support your child's academic and social success within their school environment.

  • IEP goal alignment
  • Classroom behavior support
  • Staff & teacher consultation
  • Social skills development
👥

BCBA Supervision & Consultation

All programs are designed and overseen by Board Certified Behavior Analysts who provide ongoing supervision, data review, and program updates to ensure your child's continued progress.

  • Comprehensive assessments
  • Data-driven decision making
  • Monthly caregiver meetings
  • Transition planning support
