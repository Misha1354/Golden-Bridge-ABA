What Is Home-Based ABA Therapy?

Home-based ABA therapy is one-on-one Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) delivered in your child's home — where learning happens inside the real moments of daily life: mealtimes, transitions, sibling play, getting dressed, settling down for sleep. A Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) designs an individualized program, and a trained Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) runs daily sessions, with regular BCBA supervision and parent coaching built in.

One-on-One in Your Cambridge Home

Sessions take place in the spaces your child knows best. Skills built at the kitchen table or in the playroom carry over faster than skills built in a clinic.

BCBA-Designed, RBT-Delivered

A Board Certified Behavior Analyst writes the plan, sets the goals, and supervises every case. A trained RBT runs your child's home-based sessions on a consistent schedule.

Built Around Your Family

Goals reflect what actually matters to your family — communicating wants, handling transitions, joining siblings in play, building independence with daily routines.

Home-Based ABA Therapy in Cambridge — Common Questions

What Cambridge neighborhoods do you serve for in-home ABA therapy?

We provide in-home ABA therapy throughout Cambridge, including Harvard Square, Central Square, Kendall Square, Inman Square, Porter Square, Cambridgeport, East Cambridge, North Cambridge, West Cambridge, Riverside, Mid-Cambridge, and Agassiz.

Do you work with Cambridge Public Schools (CPS)?

Yes. Our BCBAs and RBTs collaborate with Cambridge Public Schools and private schools across the city to support students with autism, with goals aligned to each student's IEP.

Is home-based ABA therapy in Cambridge covered by MassHealth?

Yes. MassHealth covers ABA therapy for children with autism throughout Cambridge. We're also in-network with Blue Cross Blue Shield of MA, Tufts Health Plan, Harvard Pilgrim, Aetna, and Cigna. We verify your benefits at no cost.

How quickly can my child start home-based ABA therapy in Cambridge?

After a free consultation, intake and insurance verification typically take 1–3 weeks. Once approved, we match your child with a Cambridge-area therapist and begin sessions on your schedule.
